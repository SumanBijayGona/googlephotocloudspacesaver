Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Photo Cloud Space Saver ("the app", "we", "our") is a browser-based tool that helps you download your Google Photos to your local device and free up cloud storage space.

1. What data we access

The app requests access to the following Google account data:

  • Google Photos library — to list and download your photos and videos.
  • Google Drive storage metadata — to display your current cloud storage usage. We only read quota information; we do not access any Drive files.
  • Basic profile (name, email, profile picture) — to display your account in the app header.

2. How your data is used

  • Your photos are downloaded directly from Google to your device. They are never uploaded to, stored on, or transmitted through any third-party server.
  • We do not collect, store, sell, or share any of your personal data or photos.
  • Sync progress is stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB to avoid re-downloading photos you've already saved.

3. No server involved

Photo Cloud Space Saver is a fully client-side web application. It runs entirely in your browser. There is no backend server, no database, and no analytics tracking.

4. Third-party services

  • Google OAuth 2.0 — used for authentication. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim — used for reverse geocoding GPS coordinates in photo EXIF data to generate location-based filenames. Only approximate coordinates are sent; no photos are transmitted.

5. Data retention

No data is retained by us. Locally stored sync progress (in your browser) can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this page.

6. Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
